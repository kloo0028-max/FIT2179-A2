Blood Donation analysis in Malaysia

How has blood donation changed over time?

Where are donations coming from, and who is donating?

When do people donate, and are new donors growing?

Donations peaked in August 2020 and mid 2022, while 2021 showed the
lowest volumes overall, likely due to the COVID-19 disruptions. Since 2022,
volumes have steadily recovered and stabilised.

The overall trend of the recruitment of new donors shows an overall
decline over the years. It is seen that due to the COVID-19
restrictions in 2021, donation centres operated at reduced capacities nationwide.
